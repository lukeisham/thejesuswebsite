About

AI and the Jesus Website

The Jesus Website is a historical record of Jesus that allows the evidence to be viewed and searched in a variety of ways. 'Records' are the key data element — each one represents a piece of historical evidence such as a manuscript, inscription, artefact or literary reference.

Records can be explored through:

  • Maps — Geographic views of Galilee, Jerusalem, Judea, the Levant and Rome
  • Timeline — Chronological navigation from the birth of Jesus through the early church
  • Ardor Diagram — A branching tree that maps the relationships between sources and attestations

Additional pages provide contextual essays, responses to common challenges, a curated collection of resources, and a Wikipedia article ranking.
